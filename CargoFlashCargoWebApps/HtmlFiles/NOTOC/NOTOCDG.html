I certify that this NOTOC has been prepared in compliance with the procedures for the acceptance and dispatch of dangerous good as detailed in the DG & safely Dangerous Goods Manual)

1.A NOTOC to be issued whenever dangerous goods(DGs) or other special lead items are to be carried on DC & safety aircraft.
2. The NOTOC form is to be prepared by the freight handling agent contracted to dispact consignment on DG & safety when DGs and/or special load are consignee as freight Or by loading master when items of special load checked in be carried.
3. The freight agent must print and sign his/her name in the DGs preparation certification field in the top right hand section of the form whenever freight load containing dangerous goods are dispatched.
4. The person responsible for entering the special load details must annolate their name in applicable name field of the special load section of the form. Two fields are provided for this one for use by the freight agent and one for the loading master(as required)
5.Before loading the loading master is to check the dangerous goods items for evidence of any non normal leatures such as leakage or damage. After loading on the aircraft , the person is responsible for supervising loading operations in to the print and sign their name in the certiviction field in the bottom right hand section of the form certifying that the items have been loaded in the location specified on the form.
6. The NOTOC is to be given to the captain who is certify acceptance of the NOTOC in the bottom left hand corner of the form.
